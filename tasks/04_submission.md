## 査読対応の準備

### 査読結果の種類と対応

| 結果 | 意味 | 対応 |
|------|------|------|
| Accept | 採択 | 校正対応 |
| Minor Revision | 軽微な修正 | 通常1-2週間以内に対応 |
| Major Revision | 大幅な修正 | 追加解析が必要な場合あり |
| Reject with Resubmission | 再投稿可 | 大幅改訂後に再投稿 |
| Reject | 却下 | 別ジャーナルへ投稿 |

### Response Letter（回答書）テンプレート

```
Dear Editor and Reviewers,

We would like to thank the editor and reviewers for their constructive
comments and suggestions. We have carefully revised our manuscript according
to the comments. Below, we provide point-by-point responses to each comment.

The reviewers' comments are in bold, and our responses are in regular font.
Changes in the manuscript are highlighted in [yellow/track changes].

---

REVIEWER 1

**Comment 1: [査読者のコメントをそのまま引用]**

Response: [回答を記載]

We have revised the manuscript as follows (page X, lines XX-XX):
"[修正した文章を引用]"

**Comment 2: [査読者のコメントをそのまま引用]**

Response: [回答を記載]

---

REVIEWER 2

**Comment 1: [査読者のコメントをそのまま引用]**

Response: [回答を記載]

---

We hope that our revisions adequately address the reviewers' concerns.
We believe the manuscript has been substantially improved and is now
suitable for publication in [Journal Name].

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Corresponding Author Name]
```

### 改訂版作成の方針

**改訂作業チェックリスト**
- [ ] 全コメントをリスト化
- [ ] コメントの優先度を設定
- [ ] 対応方針を決定（Accept/Partially Accept/Respectfully Disagree）
- [ ] 追加解析の実施（必要な場合）
- [ ] 原稿の修正
- [ ] 変更箇所のハイライト/Track Changes
- [ ] Response Letterの作成
- [ ] 共著者の確認

**改訂対応記録表**
| Reviewer | Comment# | 内容要約 | 対応 | 状態 |
|----------|---------|---------|------|------|
| R1 | 1 | | Accept/Partial/Disagree | [ ] |
| R1 | 2 | | Accept/Partial/Disagree | [ ] |
| R2 | 1 | | Accept/Partial/Disagree | [ ] |

**コメントへの対応パターン**
```
□ Accept（受け入れ）:
"We agree with this comment and have revised the manuscript accordingly."

□ Partially Accept（部分的受け入れ）:
"We partially agree with this comment. We have [対応内容]. However,
we respectfully maintain [維持する点] because [理由]."

□ Respectfully Disagree（丁重に反論）:
"We appreciate this comment. However, we respectfully disagree because
[理由]. [根拠となるデータや文献を提示]."
```
